The Rules of Engagement

The objective is simple: Checkmate the opponent's King. This means the King is under attack and has no legal move to escape.

  • Pawns: Move forward one square, but capture diagonally. On their first move, they can jump two squares.
  • Rooks: Move unlimited squares horizontally or vertically.
  • Knights: Move in an "L" shape (two squares one way, then one square perpendicular). They are the only piece that can jump over others.
  • Bishops: Move unlimited squares diagonally.
  • Queen: The most powerful piece. Combines the power of the Rook and Bishop.
  • King: Moves one square in any direction. Protect him at all costs.

Special moves like Castling, En Passant, and Promotion add depth to the strategy. You'll learn them as you play.
